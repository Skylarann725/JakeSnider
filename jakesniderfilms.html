About Jake Snider Films

Jake Snider Films is a video production company specializing in boat to boat shooting and action sports. We are experienced at handling shoots big or small, on or off the water. With high quality gear and a hardworking attitude, we work hard to bring your vision to life. Our company is based in central Florida but we are ready to travel anywhere for the needs of our clients. The majority of our work is done on production day but we are willing and able to help with pre-production and with the final editing as well. Our company has done work for companies, such as Boating Magazine, Wakeboard Magazine, Nautique Boats, HO-Sports Waterskis, Flying Magazine, Scuba Diving Magazine, as well as individual athletes.


"We work hard to bring your vision to life."

Meet the Man Behind the Camera

Jake grew up in northern Illinois where his hobbies were waterskiing, riding dirt bikes, skateboarding, and snowboarding. Action sports are in his blood. Jake completed a certificate program in video production in Spring of 2013 and started his career in the wedding industry. With a real passion for towed water sports and boating, the short summers in Illinois just weren’t enough, and a need for longer summers became necessary. In June of 2013, Jake made the move to central Florida to start doing video work in the boating and action sports industry as an apprentice to Sean Kilgus. Jake moved on to run his own company after a year of working alongside Sean. Jake understands the athletes desire for every trick to look its best, he sees the beauty of boats, and he is ready to give his all at every shoot.
